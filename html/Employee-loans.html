Employee Loan

Financial Ecosystem Employee Loan is designed for salaried workers in both private and public sectors. Repayment is made through convenient monthly salary deductions. We partner with employers to provide accessible loans and financial wellness programs that help employees manage debt, improve credit, and build lasting savings habits.

  • Fast disbursement within 24–48 hours
  • Repayment through salary deductions
  • No collateral required
  • Improves credit score and savings habits
  • Instant access to your money
  • Transparent terms
  • Competitive rates

Requirement

  • Proof of employment (e.g. ID card or letter)
  • Recent payslips (last 3–6 months)
  • Valid means of identification
  • Bank statements (3–6 months)
  • Employer partnership or consent
  • BVN and active salary account
