LPO Financing

The Financial Ecosystem LPO (Local Purchase Order) Financing is designed to empower businesses and contractors by providing upfront capital to execute purchase orders from reputable companies or government agencies. It bridges the cash flow gap, ensuring seamless delivery and contract fulfillment.

  • Support for SMEs and contractors handling large projects
  • No need for full collateral
  • Improves cash flow and business credibility
  • Flexible repayment terms

Requirement

  • Valid and verifiable LPO from a reputable organization
  • CAC registration and relevant business documents
  • Company bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Quotation or invoice from supplier (if applicable)
  • Valid means of identification of company directors
  • Execution capability proof (e.g., past contracts, capacity letter)
