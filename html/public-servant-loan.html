Employee Loan

The Financial Ecosystem Public Servant Loan is a tailored loan package designed for government employees across federal, state, or local levels. It provides accessible and reliable financing backed by payroll deductions, enabling public workers to meet personal, family, or emergency needs without financial strain.

  • Flexible repayment via automatic salary deductions
  • Quick approval process with minimal paperwork
  • Competitive interest rates tailored for public workers
  • No collateral required
  • Loan top-up options after partial repayment
  • Improves financial wellness and credit profile

Requirement

  • Must be a confirmed public servant (federal, state, or LGA)
  • Recent payslips (last 3 months)
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Employment confirmation letter
  • Official email or employer contact for verification
